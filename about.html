Why Join Power Yourself Up?

Ever wanted to save the world? Ever wanted to seek revenge on your enemies? Ever wanted to walk the grey line in between? Here at Power Yourself Up, we don't judge you on what you want to do with your powers, we just want you to embrace them! You don't even have to claim your powers for good or evil, you can claim them for whatever you want! Do you want the power to instantly cook meals and never have to worry about that again? We are here for you! Do you want the power to steal from the rich? We are SO here for you! Claim your powers today and join!




If you would like to join, fill out the form below and we will get back to you.

Make sure to read the conditions of membership before filling the form out!
We'll never share your email with anyone else.

Conditions of membership:

1) At meetings, members must be cordial with each other at all times. No fights, explosions, maiming, killing, or weapons, etc., are allowed at meetings.
2) Power Board members are granted immunity from all members
3) Absolutely no world ending activities will be tolerated. We condone many villainous behaviors, but we all need a place to live. If world-ending activites are sought by any individual, member or not, all members are required to come to arms against the world-ending threat.
4) Any violation of the above terms will result in a case brought before the Power Board, in which verdict and sentencing will be decided by majority vote.
