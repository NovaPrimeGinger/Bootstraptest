The Scarlet Wizard The Scarlet Wizard is a Nexus being, meaning only one of him exists in the entire multiverse. The powers of the Scarlet Wizard lie dormant in the host body until they are ready to emerge. Once the Scarlet Wizard emerges, almsot no power in the multiverse can defy that of his, as he taps into Chaos Magic, which allows the user to instantly create, destroy, and manipulate matter.
Black Bolt Black Bolt is the leader of the Inhumans, a group of humans with dormant genes activated at a pivotal point in their lives. Black Bolt's powers are a gifgt and a curse, as he is extremely powerful, but he cannot speak unless he wants to decimate something. His vocal chords are extremely powerful and create sonic booms so powerful they could tear apart almost anything.
Persephone Persephone is perhaps one of the most gifted telepathic and telekinetic individuals in the world. At birth, the Goddess Bast made her the avatar cat for her spirit on this plane. Given that, Persephone can communicate telepathically with other cats and humans, no matter what language. She is a very passive being, preferring belly rubs to fighting anything, so she usually uses her telekinetic abilities to get into places she shouldn't.
